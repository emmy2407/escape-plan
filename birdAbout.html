Welcome to Escape Plan Game.

Escape Plan is a passion game whereby the player is represented as the white box at
the left side of the game play area. The yellow box moving represents the enemies or
also called opponents. Whenever you move and you touch any of the yellow box, your
health life reduce by -1. Your initial health life is 20 in total and the initial speed is 5.
As you pass each level the speed increase by 1 and your health life also increased but
when you lose in a move ur health life reduce by -1.

How To Play.

Left click or click on the white box to move your player. Try to reach the goal without
being caught by enemies.

Music Mode.

If you want to play the music it is optional.
Touch the play button.
